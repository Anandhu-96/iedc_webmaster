About The Event

Innovation & Entrepreneurship Development Cell

IEDC RIT is the premier student-run hub for fostering innovation, entrepreneurship, and technical excellence at Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Technology. Our mission is to nurture the next generation of tech leaders and problem solvers.

Ideathon 2024

As part of Prominence 2024, the annual technical fest of RIT, we present the Ideathon - a platform for students to showcase their innovative ideas and solutions to real-world problems.

This 24-hour hackathon will challenge participants to think creatively, collaborate effectively, and develop prototypes for their innovative ideas in technology and entrepreneurship.

Event Details

Theme

"Sustainable Technology for a Better Future" - Innovate solutions that address pressing environmental and social challenges through technology.

Problem Statements

  • Waste Management Solutions
  • Renewable Energy Applications
  • Smart City Technologies
  • Healthcare Innovations
  • Open Innovation Track

Prizes

  • 1st Prize: ₹25,000 + Incubation Support
  • 2nd Prize: ₹15,000 + Mentorship
  • 3rd Prize: ₹10,000
  • Special Category Awards

Eligibility & Rules

  • Open to all undergraduate and postgraduate students
  • Teams of 2-4 participants
  • Original ideas only - no plagiarism
  • Judging criteria: Innovation, Feasibility, Impact, Presentation
